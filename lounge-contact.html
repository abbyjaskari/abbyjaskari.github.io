Campground News

We have RV and tent sites ready!

Sleeper cabin now open to rent!
Check out the gallery and reservation pages for pictures and rates for this cozy lakeside sleeper cabin!

Our beautiful campground is located on Big Lake in Cloquet, Minnesota - a quick 30 minutes from Duluth/Superior and 2 hours north of the Twin Cities.

The Lounge Tavern also serves as our campground office. Stop in for a drink, a bite to eat, some ice or firewood, or to simply hang out and shoot pool.

The Lounge is open all year round! Keep watch for our summer, fall and winter events!
